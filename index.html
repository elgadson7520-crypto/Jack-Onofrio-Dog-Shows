Important

Exhibitor Notices

Fax/Email Entries No Longer Accepted

Effective immediately, fax and email entries are not acceptable and will not be processed. Online entries and checks by mail remain fully supported. Paper entries with credit card information will be shredded upon delivery to protect your security. We always accept entries with check payment at any of our shows.

Mail Delivery Delays

We have received multiple reports of first class mail taking up to 10 days for delivery. Club/show entry forms are available for printing from the Upcoming Shows page. Please fill out and mail entries as early as possible to avoid late arrivals due to postal delivery timeframes.

Foreign Dog Importation Updates

Exhibitors of foreign dogs should visit the CDC importation page for the most recent updates regarding bringing a dog into the United States. Requirements and regulations are subject to change.

Show Closing Times

All shows close at the listed day, date, and time (Central) as published on this site. Entries received after the closing deadline cannot be accepted. Plan your submissions accordingly.

Dog Registration Requirement

Reminder: As of January 1, 2010, all dogs must be fully registered by the day of the event you want to enter and compete. Use AKC's online registration service for fast and easy registration.
